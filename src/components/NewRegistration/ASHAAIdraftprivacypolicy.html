2. Key Definitions

  • "Personal Information": Information that can identify you, such as your name, contact info, and other unique identifiers.
  • "PHR Data": Personal Health Record Data, including medical history, diagnoses, treatments, prescriptions, lab results, etc.
  • "Aggregate Data": Data that has been de-identified so it cannot reasonably be used to identify an individual.
  • "Provider": A healthcare provider, practice, or hospital that you authorize to share health data with the Service.
  • "Authorized Individual": A parent, guardian, or another person who accesses your or your child's health data with your permission. (Note: Asha AI currently does not provide a feature for sharing your entire profile to another user's account, but parents may create sub-profiles for their dependents.)

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Account Information

  • Registration: We collect your name, email address, phone number, birthdate, username, and password when you create an account.
  • Child/Dependent Profiles: Parents/guardians can create sub-profiles for minors or dependents under their main account; information in these sub-profiles is also covered by this Policy.

3.2 PHR / Health Data

  • User-Provided Records: You may upload or manually enter health information (e.g., vitals, medications, symptoms, lab results).
  • Provider Imports: At your direction, the App may connect to a Provider's portal to retrieve your medical records. We only do this with your explicit permission and login credentials.
  • No Guarantee of Accuracy: We do not originate or verify the accuracy of medical records. We simply store and present them for your convenience. Any AI-generated insights are informational and not guaranteed accurate.

3.3 AI & Voice Services

  • We use third-party AI and voice APIs (e.g., from AWS, Google, Microsoft Azure, OpenAI, Perplexity, ElevenLabs) to enable features such as search, data summarization, text-to-speech (TTS), and speech-to-text (STT).
  • Identifiable Data may be sent to these external services if you use AI-based features. By using these features, you consent to such data transfers.
  • We do not control how these third parties handle data once they process it, though we aim to use vendors that limit or delete logs. If you prefer not to share data with these providers, do not use AI-powered features.

3.4 Usage Data

  • Technical Details: We collect IP addresses, device types, operating system info, and pages viewed to analyze trends and improve stability.
  • Cookies & Tokens: We may use cookies, web beacons, or session tokens to maintain sessions and remember your preferences.

3.5 Payment Information

  • If you purchase subscriptions or upgrades, a third-party payment processor handles your billing info. We do not store full credit card numbers.

3.6 Communications

  • If you contact us by email or other channels, we keep copies of your messages and related metadata for support and troubleshooting.

3.7 Activity Logs

  • We may track when PHR Data is accessed, modified, or shared (if that becomes a future feature) to maintain security and audit trails.
